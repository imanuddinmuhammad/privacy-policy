Privacy Policy for Prayer Times App

Last updated: January 2024

Information Collection and Use

The Prayer Times App does not collect any personal information. We only store your preferences (such as location settings, prayer time adjustments, and sound settings) locally on your device.

Location Information

The app requires location information only for calculating accurate prayer times. This information is stored locally and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Data Storage

  • Prayer time settings
  • Location preferences
  • Sound settings
  • Iqomah duration settings
  • Marquee text settings

All data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
